# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project exists to help engineers build better agent systems. Contributions, discussions, and feedback are welcome from everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive. Critique ideas, not people.
- Be specific. "This example is misleading because X" is useful. "This is bad" is not.
- Be respectful of different engineering contexts. What works in a startup may not work in a bank, and vice versa.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, spam, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to sunil@sunilprakash.com. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly. Maintainers reserve the right to remove content or ban participants who violate this code.
